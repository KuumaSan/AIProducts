# 巴菲特致股东的信 — 精读笔记

来源：Berkshire Hathaway Shareholder Letters (1965-2024)

---

## 单元 1：1977-1980年（第1-50页）

### 核心年份主题
- **1977**：初步建立投资哲学框架，保险业务高速增长
- **1978**：多元化业务版图成型，SAFECO投资逻辑深化
- **1979**：通胀威胁下的实际回报思考，长期债券的陷阱
- **1980**：Non-controlled ownership earnings概念，GEICO案例

### 关键投资原则

**选股四标准（反复强化）：**
1. 可理解的业务
2. 良好的长期前景
3. 诚实且有能力的管理层
4. 非常有吸引力的价格

**ROE > EPS：** "The primary test of managerial economic performance is the achievement of a high earnings rate on equity capital employed, not the achievement of consistent gains in EPS."

**集中持仓：** "We try to avoid buying a little of this or that when we are only lukewarm about the business or its price."

**非控制权投资可能优于全资收购：** 同样优质资产，市场上以折价买入少数股权，比谈判收购100%更划算。

**Tailwinds vs Headwinds：** "When a management with a reputation for brilliance tackles a business with a reputation for poor fundamental economics, it is the reputation of the business that remains intact."

**Turnarounds seldom turn：** 同样的精力放在好企业上回报远高于放在差企业的便宜货上。

### 通胀与真实回报
- "Investor's Misery Index" = 通胀率 + 税率
- 当此指数 > ROE时，即使消费为零，购买力也在缩水
- 长期固定利率债券是通胀时代最大的定价错误

### 保险业务智慧
- 承保纪律 > 保费规模
- Float = 低成本/零成本投资资金
- 高利率引诱保险公司接受承保亏损 → 恶性循环

### 经典金句
> "We welcome lower market prices of stocks we own as an opportunity to acquire even more of a good thing at a better price."

> "Neither a short-term borrower nor a long-term lender be."

> "You may feel richer, but you won't eat richer."（论通胀对购买力的侵蚀）

## 单元 2：1981-1983年（第51-100页）

### 核心年份主题
- **1981**：Non-controlled ownership earnings深化、收购行为的三大动机批判、Equity Value-Added危机、通胀tapeworm
- **1982**：放弃传统yardstick、Accounting vs Economic Earnings、保险行业commodity+overcapacity框架、发行股权的原则
- **1983**：管理原则宣言（catechism）、Nebraska Furniture Mart收购、Book Value vs Intrinsic Business Value、Goodwill

### 关键投资原则

**Toad/Prince 理论：** "Investors can always buy toads at the going price for toads. If investors instead bankroll princesses who wish to pay double for the right to kiss the toad, those kisses had better pack some real dynamite."

**Noah Principle：** "Predicting rain doesn't count, building arks does."

**Empire vs Citizenry：** "The empire would have been larger, but the citizenry would have been poorer."

**非控股投资的优势：** 
- 纠错更快
- 选择范围更广（几乎所有大公司 vs 极少数可全资收购的）
- "Lack of control, in effect, often has turned out to be an economic plus."

**Equity Value-Added 框架（极重要）：**
- 当 passive bond yields > after-tax ROE 时，企业不值 100 cents on the dollar
- 通胀是 "gigantic corporate tapeworm"，先于一切消耗投资本金
- 低回报企业被迫留存收益 — 不是因为 attractive，恰恰 because unattractive

**保险行业结构分析：**
- Commodity product + overcapacity = 利润灾难
- 行业改善只能靠 supply withdrawal（需要 mega-disaster 等 shock）
- GEICO 是例外：wide and sustainable cost advantage

**发行股权的铁律：** "We will not issue shares unless we receive as much intrinsic business value as we give."
- 用低估的股票买全价的资产 = 给出 $2 获得 $1
- "Managers who want to expand their domain at the expense of owners might better consider a career in government."

**Economic Earnings vs Accounting Earnings：**
- "Accounting numbers are the beginning, not the end, of business valuation."
- 会计准则可能让多分红反而显示更高报表利润

### 经典金句
> "It has struck me that all men's misfortunes spring from the single cause that they are unable to stay quietly in one room." — Pascal

> "Don't ask the barber whether you need a haircut."

> "Facts do not cease to exist, either because they are unpleasant or because they are ignored."

> "A too-high purchase price for the stock of an excellent company can undo the effects of a subsequent decade of favorable business developments."

## 单元 3：1983-1985年（第101-150页）

### 核心年份主题
- **1983（附录）**：Economic Goodwill 理论——价值投资基石文章
- **1984**：WPPSS 债券分析框架、股利政策系统论述、保险准备金错误反思
- **1985**：规模制约卓越回报、股票回购 vs Greenmail

### 关键新洞见

**Economic Goodwill 框架（极重要）：**
- 经济商誉 = 超额回报的资本化价值（ROE 远超市场平均的部分）
- 来源：consumer franchise（定价权基于价值而非成本）
- 通胀下，经济商誉是 "the gift that keeps giving"——轻资产高 ROE 企业只需少量追加资本即可翻倍盈利
- 颠覆传统智慧：重资产企业不是通胀保护，轻资产 franchise 才是
- "A good business is not always a good purchase – although it's a good place to look for one."

**用企业分析法看债券（WPPSS 案例）：**
- 把债券当作一个"企业"——固定税后收益 16.3%，同类经营性企业需付 2.5-3 亿才有同等收益
- "Investment is most intelligent when it is most businesslike." — Ben Graham

**股利政策铁律：**
- 每留存 $1 至少创造 $1 市值，否则应分红
- "Restricted earnings"（维持竞争地位所需的强制留存）不算真利润
- Pro-Am 效应：核心业务掩盖资本配置的反复失败

**规模的铁律：** "Growth eventually dampens exceptional economics. None that I know of has managed to keep earning 20%+ ROE on $1B+ equity while reinvesting all earnings."

**保险行业 Walking Dead：** 资不抵债的保险公司不会倒闭（保费在前赔付在后），反而加倍承保压低行业定价。

**Invisible Foot：** "Casino-type markets and hair-trigger investment management act as an invisible foot that trips up and slows down a forward-moving economy."

### 经典金句
> "During inflation, Goodwill is the gift that keeps giving."

> "If you have a harem of forty women, you never get to know any of them very well." — Billy Rose

> "A hyperactive stock market is the pickpocket of enterprise."

> "Failure seems to go to their heads."（论 CEO 收购成功后更大胆地犯错）

## 单元 4：1985-1986年（第151-200页）

### 核心年份主题
- **1985**：纺织业关闭复盘、Stock Options 系统批判、激励薪酬设计、Capital Cities/ABC
- **1986**：CEO 只有两个职责、恐惧与贪婪、Permanent Holdings 永不出售原则、保险周期量化

### 关键新洞见

**"船"比"划船技术"重要 100 倍：** "Should you find yourself in a chronically-leaking boat, energy devoted to changing vessels is likely to be more productive than energy devoted to patching leaks."

**Commodity Business 的站脚尖悖论：** 个体层面合理的资本开支，集体层面相互抵消 → 必须用博弈论检验：竞争对手都做同样投资后，行业价格基线下移多少？

**Tangible Assets 价值完全取决于 Earning Power：** 纺织设备原价 $13M，账面 $866K，替换成本 $30-50M，拍卖所得 $163K。两条报纸路线的 goodwill > 这堆实体资产。

**Stock Options 批判：**
- 未计入 retained earnings 的自动增值和 carrying cost of capital
- 管理层不会给 outsider 同条件 option，却给自己
- Berkshire 替代方案：按各单元自身业绩、不看股价、不封顶

**CEO 只有两个职责：** 吸引和保留优秀管理者 + 资本配置

**保险产能是"态度性"的：** 不像钢厂需要建设，资本一到位产能立即释放 → 超额利润窗口极短

**"恐惧与贪婪"：** "We simply attempt to be fearful when others are greedy and to be greedy only when others are fearful."（写于 1987 年初，黑色星期一前）

**Permanent Holdings 原则：** Capital Cities/ABC、GEICO、Washington Post — 即使显著高估也不卖。超越 Graham 式"买便宜卖贵"。

**资金配置不是选"好"与"坏"：** 而是选"最不令人反感的"选项。Doing nothing 也是主动选择。

### 经典金句
> "No matter how great the talent or effort, some things just take time: you can't produce a baby in one month by getting nine women pregnant."

> "Marrying for money — probably a bad idea under any circumstances, but absolute madness if you are already rich."

> "Bull markets can obscure mathematical laws, but they cannot repeal them."

## 单元 5：1987-1989年（第201-250页）

### 核心年份主题
- **1986附录**：Owner Earnings 估值框架（最重要原创概念之一）
- **1987**：Mr. Market 寓言、Portfolio Insurance 批判、Permanent Holdings 正式确立、CEO 资本配置能力
- **1988**：GAAP 定位、Debt 政策、Growing Capital Base 量化、Convertible Preferred

### 关键新洞见

**Owner Earnings 框架（极重要）：**
- = Reported earnings + D&A - 维持性 CapEx
- 维持性 CapEx 是猜测，但 "I would rather be vaguely right than precisely wrong"
- "Cash Flow" 不扣维持性 CapEx，对制造/零售/采掘毫无意义
- "Though dentists correctly claim that if you ignore your teeth they'll go away, the same is not true for maintenance capex."

**Mr. Market：** "He is there to serve you, not to guide you." 扑克法则："If you've been in the game 30 minutes and you don't know who the patsy is, you're the patsy."

**Franchise 与税收差异化：** 强 franchise + 不受管制 = 减税利益全留股东；弱 franchise = 最终传导到价格

**保险业两大差异化：** 财务实力（赔付确定性）+ 对 volume 完全无所谓

**CEO 资本配置悖论：** 因营销/生产/工程能力升任 CEO，却要做从未训练过的资本配置决策。"It's as if the final step for a highly-talented musician was not to perform at Carnegie Hall but to be named Chairman of the Federal Reserve."

**企业稳定性 = 超额回报：** Fortune 1000 中仅 25 家 10 年 ROE>20%，特征：低杠杆、业务平凡、十年不变。"Severe change and exceptional returns usually don't mix."

**Debt 政策：** 融资应 anticipate need 而非 react to need。"If you want to shoot rare, fast-moving elephants, you should always carry a loaded gun."

**Portfolio Insurance 荒谬：** "After buying a farm, would a rational owner next order his real estate agent to start selling off pieces of it whenever a neighboring property was sold at a lower price?"

### 经典金句
> "I would rather be vaguely right than precisely wrong." — Keynes

> "It has been far safer to steal large sums with a pen than small sums with a gun."

> "How much is two plus two?" Replies the cooperative accountant, "What number did you have in mind?"

> "I have always depended on the kindness of strangers." — Blanche DuBois（论美国贸易赤字）
